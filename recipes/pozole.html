Pozole Recipe

Description

Pozole is a traditional mexican dish, mostly served during Mexico's Independence Day, its preparation may vary depending on the region.

Ingredients:

  • Maiz pozolero
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Morita pepper
  • Guajillo pepper
  • Cooking oil

Preparation:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water
  2. Mince and fry with oil the peppers with the onion
  3. Blend the water with the pepper mixture and add a tooth of garlic
  4. Add everything to a pot
  5. Add maiz pozolero
  6. Let it sit in medium heat for 20 mins
